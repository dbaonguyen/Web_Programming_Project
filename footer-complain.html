DISPUTE RESOLUTION PROCESS, COMPLAINTS

is explained and adjusted in accordance with the Regulations for the Management of Lazada Marketplace Operations dated June 3, 2022, as amended and supplemented from time to time ("Operating Regulations"). Capitalized terms in this dispute resolution and complaint process shall have the meanings as defined in the Operating Regulations.


Process for Domestic Seller's Products

(a) Lazada and the Seller are responsible for receiving complaints and assisting Customers regarding transactions on the Lazada Marketplace. In the event of a dispute, Lazada emphasizes negotiated solutions and mediation between parties to maintain Member confidence in the quality of Lazada Marketplace services and follows these steps:

  1. Step 1: Customers file complaints about Products through the customer care hotline at 19001007 or through the online chat function at www.lazada.vn/contact.
  2. Step 2: Lazada's customer care department will receive Customer complaints and transfer them to the Seller. Depending on the nature and severity of the complaint, Lazada may take specific actions to assist the Customer in resolving the dispute. For transactions involving more than two (2) parties on the Lazada Marketplace, Lazada will be the point of contact for receiving and resolving complaints from Buyers.
  3. Step 3: In cases beyond Lazada's capacity and jurisdiction, Customers may request that the matter be resolved by the relevant state authority.

(b) Lazada respects and diligently complies with consumer protection laws. Therefore, Sellers are required to provide complete, accurate, truthful, and detailed information about their Products listed on the Lazada Marketplace. Any fraudulent or deceptive business practices are condemned and fully liable under the law.

(c) All parties, including Sellers, Customers, and logistics providers, have a responsibility to actively participate in resolving the disputed issues. Relevant parties must provide documents and authenticating materials related to the conflict. Lazada will be responsible for providing information related to the parties involved in the dispute if requested by the relevant parties.

(d) After the parties have resolved the dispute, they must report back to Lazada. In cases where the transaction dispute results from Seller's fault, Lazada may issue warnings, suspend accounts, or transfer the case to the relevant state authority, depending on the severity of the violation. Lazada may also terminate and remove all listings of the Seller's Products that violate Lazada Marketplace rules. Additionally, the Seller is responsible for reasonable compensation or reimbursement to the Customer based on mutual agreement.

(e) If, through mutual agreement, the dispute cannot be resolved, the parties have the right to seek intervention from the relevant state authority to protect their legal interests.


Process for Products of Foreign Sellers

(a) Lazada will represent Foreign Sellers to receive and resolve Customer complaints related to the Products of Foreign Sellers sold on the Lazada Platform.

(b) Conditions for Lazada to resolve Customer complaints:

  • Customers purchased Products from Foreign Sellers on the Lazada Platform and have paid the purchase price but (A) the expected delivery time has passed, and the Customer has not received the Product; or (B) the order has been updated to a successful delivery status, but the Customer has not received the Product; (C) the Customer is dissatisfied with the Product and wishes to return it in accordance with Lazada's return policy; and (D) the Product significantly differs from the information provided by the Foreign Seller on the Lazada Platform.
  • A Product is considered significantly different in the following cases:
    • The condition of the Product does not match the description.
    • The wrong Product was delivered.
    • The Product is missing parts or components.
    • The Product is counterfeit or fake.
    • The Product is damaged or broken.
    • The packaging of the Product is torn, deformed, damaged, or broken.
    • The Product has technical defects.
  • Customers may file complaints with Lazada starting from the day following the expected delivery date if they have not received the Product or from the date the order has been updated to a successful delivery status. In cases of a significant difference in the Product, complaints must be submitted to Lazada within fifteen (15) days from the date the order was successfully delivered.
  • In cases where Customers pay with a credit card and the issuing bank has initiated a chargeback for the Product's purchase price before or after Lazada receives the Customer's complaint, Lazada will not accept or continue to process (as applicable) the Customer's complaint and will notify the Customer accordingly.

(c) The process for receiving and resolving complaints is as follows:

  1. Step 1: Customers file complaints about Products through the customer care hotline at 19006509 or through the online chat function at https://www.lazada.vn/contact.
  2. Step 2: If the Customer's complaint does not meet the conditions specified in section (b) above, Lazada will not continue to process the complaint and will notify the Customer. If the Customer's complaint meets the conditions specified in section (b) above, Lazada will make maximum efforts to respond to the complaint resolution result within forty-five (45) days or a longer period (if necessary) from the date of receiving the complaint.
  3. Step 3: After the complaint resolution period ends, Lazada will inform the Customer of the complaint resolution result. In cases where the Customer's complaint is accepted or has a basis, Lazada will refund the purchase price of the Product to the Customer. In cases where the Customer's complaint has no basis, Lazada will notify the Customer.

(d) To resolve complaints, Lazada may request the Customer to provide additional information and/or relevant documents related to the complaint. If the Customer fails to provide the information and/or documents requested by Lazada within five (5) days from the date of the request, or if the information and/or documents provided by the Customer to Lazada are fake or altered, at Lazada's sole discretion, Lazada will terminate the Customer's complaint resolution and notify the Customer.
